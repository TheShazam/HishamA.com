Artificial Intelligence(AI) HackAI 2018 Mail Sorting
Artificial Intelligence(AI)

HackAI 2018 Mail Sorting

During Fall 2018, I had the wonderful opportunity to attend the HACKAI FALL 2018 event sponsored by the Mavericks and many other companies! For the project, we developed a Mail Sorting AI application that essentially took 300+ pictures of real packages, which were taken in black and white lightning and were tasked with locating the address all of which were in different locations. Features include,

  • Integrated YOLO algorithm to train a neural network model to recognize the locations of ROI (Regions of Interest) to translate mail packages.
  • Implemented OpenCV to perform preprocessing to increase neural network model recognition accuracy.
  • Applied Google drive to store images and training results to facilitate for Google Colab only storing during runtime.
  • Code was done in Google Colab, in Python 3.6.6 and Colab provided a Nvidia Tesla K80, with CUDA compute capability 3.7.

Won 2nd place for HACKAI 2018. An introduction to Artificial Intelligence.
